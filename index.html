SafeRoute AI

Smart Insurance for Delivery Workers

Problem

Delivery workers lose daily income due to factors like heavy rain, high pollution, and city restrictions. There is no system that automatically compensates them for this income loss.

Our Solution

SafeRoute AI provides an AI-powered parametric insurance system that:
  • Predicts risk using environmental data
  • Charges weekly premium based on risk
  • Automatically detects disruption events
  • Triggers instant payouts without manual claims

Real-Life Scenario

A delivery worker earning ₹500/day cannot work due to heavy rainfall (>20mm). SafeRoute AI detects this and automatically compensates a portion of lost income.
